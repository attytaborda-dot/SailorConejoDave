Sacred Oath

The Mission

In the name of chocolate, biscuits, and all things sugary, Sailor Conejo Dave has sworn to protect the world from the forces of evil — and also from anyone who dares put vegetables near his dessert.

Summoned from the depths of a Manchester Greggs, he travelled to Barcelona with a single purpose: to fight darkness, eat pastries, and confuse everyone with his very British accent.

His power grows exponentially in the presence of a chocolate fountain. His weakness is kale.
